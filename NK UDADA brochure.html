About Us

NK Udada Foundation is a youth-led organization in Uganda working to empower young people through education, health support, and life skills.

Our Mission

To empower and equip youth with tools needed to live with dignity and independence.

What We Do

  • Education Access Support
  • Menstrual Health Programs
  • Life Skills Training
  • Mentorship Programs
  • Community Advocacy

Who We Serve

Young girls and boys across Uganda, especially those in under-resourced communities.
